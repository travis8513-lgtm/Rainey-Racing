About Rainey Racing

Our Mission

To compete at a high level in dirt go-kart racing while building lasting partnerships with sponsors who want their brand seen, respected, and promoted both at the track and through our racing community.

Our Values

We race as a brother team, representing dedication, sportsmanship, and the competitive spirit of grassroots motorsports. Every race is an opportunity to grow, perform, and proudly represent the businesses that support us.

Our Commitment

We are committed to racing with integrity, sportsmanship, and determination while giving our sponsors strong visibility and appreciation all season long.

Why Partner With Rainey Racing?

By sponsoring Rainey Racing, your business gains visibility in front of race fans, families, teams, and local communities throughout the racing season.
